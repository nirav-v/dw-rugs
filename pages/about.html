About Me

Dominic Windey


Hometown : Jackson Hole, Wyoming
Currently Resides: San Diego, California

During the height of the COVID-19 pandemic people began to take on many new hobbies. I too was finding myself struggling to find purpose everyday while being stuck at home. One of the hobbies that stuck out to me most was rug making. I started to see rug making getting some traction and I was always so intrigued by it. Before I even purchased any of the materials, I was compiling images of what I wanted my first rug to be. After a lot of deliberation, I decided my first rug was going to be the Blonde album cover by Frank Ocean.
